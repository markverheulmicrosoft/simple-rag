SpaceX and other private companies are revolutionizing space travel through reusable rocket technology. This innovation has significantly reduced launch costs and increased access to space.

The search for exoplanets has revealed thousands of worlds beyond our solar system. Some of these planets exist in the "habitable zone" where liquid water could potentially exist on their surface.

Mars exploration continues to advance, with multiple rovers studying the red planet's geology and atmosphere. Future missions aim to search for signs of ancient microbial life and prepare for human exploration.

The James Webb Space Telescope is providing unprecedented views of distant galaxies and star formation, helping scientists understand the early universe and planetary formation.
